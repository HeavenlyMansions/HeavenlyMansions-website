About Us

HeavenlyMansions International (HMI) is a global non-profit interfaith organization dedicated to fostering peaceful coexistence through mutual respect and collaboration among diverse religious communities. We believe dialogue and education are the foundation for resolving conflict and building shared futures.

Our Programs

Interfaith Forums

Public dialogues, panel discussions, and forums for people of all faiths to engage in respectful conversation.

Peacebuilding Projects

Grassroots mediation, conflict resolution, and youth-led peace campaigns in conflict-prone areas.

Education & Training

Workshops, seminars, and leadership courses for interfaith leaders, youth, and women advocates.
